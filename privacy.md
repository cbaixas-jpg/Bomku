# Privacy Policy — Bomku

**Last updated:** April 8, 2026

Pahoex SPA ("we", "us", "our") operates the Bomku mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information you provide
We do not require you to create an account. We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or phone number.

### Information collected automatically
- **Device identifiers** (Advertising ID) used by Google AdMob to deliver ads
- **Crash reports and performance data** to improve app stability
- **Anonymous gameplay statistics** (scores, levels completed, time played)
- **In-app purchase records** processed by Google Play Billing

### Local data stored on your device
- Game progress (lives, coins, gems, scores, current level)
- Settings preferences (sound, music, vibration, language)
- VIP subscription status

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

## 2. How We Use Information

- To provide and maintain the App
- To display personalized advertisements through Google AdMob
- To process in-app purchases through Google Play Billing
- To analyze gameplay patterns and improve the App
- To detect and prevent fraud

## 3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

- **Google AdMob** — Advertising ([Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy))
- **Google Play Services** — In-app purchases, achievements ([Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy))
- **Unity Analytics / Unity IAP** — Game analytics and purchases ([Privacy Policy](https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy))

These services may collect information sent by your device, including the pages you visit, your IP address, device identifiers, and other diagnostic data.

## 4. Children's Privacy

The App is rated for ages 7+. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take action.

We comply with Google Play's Families Policy and COPPA where applicable.

## 5. In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases (gem packs and VIP subscriptions). All transactions are processed by Google Play Billing. We do not store or have access to your payment information.

VIP subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage subscriptions in your Google Play account settings.

## 6. Advertising

The App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may use your Advertising ID to show personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings:

- **Android:** Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

## 7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we process. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:
- Access the data we hold about you
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt out of personalized advertising
- Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

## 10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
